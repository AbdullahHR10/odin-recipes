Fried Rice

Home

A quick and easy fried rice recipe that’s perfect for using up leftover rice. Ready in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked white rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

How to Make

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  3. Add remaining oil, then stir-fry vegetables for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the rice and soy sauce. Stir well to combine.
  5. Return eggs to the pan, mix everything together, and season with salt, pepper, and sesame oil.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
